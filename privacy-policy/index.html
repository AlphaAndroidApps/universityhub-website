Who we are

University Hub is a student-focused academic and career support app that provides syllabus access, study materials, previous papers, AI-assisted study features, notifications, community features, and student tools. This policy applies to the University Hub Android app, its associated backend services, and the public website used to support the app.

Privacy contact: admin@universityhub.co.in

Data we collect

Depending on how you use University Hub, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account data: name, email address, sign-in credentials, Google Sign-In account details, and authentication identifiers.
  • Profile and academic data: university, college, branch, semester, regulation, profile image, biography, social links, and related profile details you choose to provide.
  • User-generated content: uploaded files, notes, PDFs, images, comments, posts, discussions, answers, messages, study content, and other materials you submit.
  • Resume and document data: personal details, education, skills, project details, resume content, and other information you enter into student tools.
  • AI feature inputs and outputs: text, files, OCR results, prompts, and generated responses when you use AI-powered summaries, question extraction, or study assistance features.
  • Notification data: push notification tokens, topic subscriptions, local reminder settings, and notification preferences.
  • Device and usage data: app interactions, crash or diagnostic signals, approximate technical device information, advertising identifiers, network details, and similar operational metadata collected by us or our service providers.
  • Locally stored data: cached study content, downloaded files, saved preferences, reminders, favorites, notes, and locally stored progress data on your device.

How we use data

  • To create and manage accounts and let users sign in with email, password, Google Sign-In, or supported guest flows.
  • To personalize academic content based on university, branch, semester, regulation, and other profile settings.
  • To host, sync, display, and manage uploaded materials, discussions, comments, posts, resumes, and other content.
  • To process files and text for study features such as OCR, AI summaries, question extraction, and related assistance tools.
  • To send push notifications, topic updates, reminders, and app-related operational messages.
  • To provide community, leaderboard, and contribution features.
  • To display ads, including rewarded and native ads, and to support monetization of the app.
  • To maintain app security, detect abuse, troubleshoot issues, improve performance, and comply with legal obligations.

How data may be shared

We do not sell personal information. We may share data in the following situations:

  • With service providers: including Google Firebase services for authentication, database, storage, messaging, and related infrastructure.
  • With advertising providers: including Google AdMob and related Google advertising services used to deliver ads.
  • With AI providers: including Google AI or Gemini-related services when you intentionally use AI-powered features.
  • With other users: when you intentionally upload materials, create posts, participate in discussion features, publish profile information, or otherwise share content in community areas.
  • For legal or safety reasons: when reasonably necessary to comply with law, enforce our terms, protect users, investigate abuse, or respond to valid legal requests.
  • During business or operational changes: if the app or related assets are transferred, data may be transferred as part of that transition subject to applicable law.

Advertising and AI features

University Hub uses Google AdMob to show ads, including native and rewarded ad formats. Ad providers may collect device-level signals such as advertising identifiers, IP address, diagnostics, and interaction data according to their own policies.

University Hub also offers AI-assisted study features. When you choose to use those tools, the text, files, prompts, OCR output, and related inputs required for the feature may be processed by Google AI or Gemini-related services to generate summaries, questions, explanations, or similar results.

If you do not want content processed for a specific AI feature, do not submit that content through the feature.

Permissions and device access

University Hub may request access to certain device features only when relevant to the feature you use:

  • Camera: to scan documents, capture images, or upload profile or study-related images.
  • Files and media access: to let you pick, upload, open, download, and manage PDFs, images, and related files.
  • Notifications: to send reminders, updates, and topic-based alerts that you choose to receive.
  • Network access: to sync content, authenticate users, serve files, fetch academic resources, display ads, and run AI features.

You can manage permissions through your device settings. Some features may not work properly if access is denied.

Retention and deletion

We retain account data, profile information, uploads, community content, and related records for as long as needed to operate the app, provide requested features, maintain security, resolve disputes, comply with law, and preserve legitimate backup or audit records.

  • Locally stored content such as downloads, reminders, caches, and preferences may remain on your device until you remove them or uninstall the app.
  • Community posts, uploads, comments, and similar content may remain visible until deleted by you, removed by administrators, or cleared according to operational needs.
  • If you want to request deletion of your account or associated data, contact admin@universityhub.co.in with enough information for us to identify your account.
  • We may retain limited information where necessary for fraud prevention, abuse prevention, legal compliance, dispute resolution, or security logs.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect data, including managed cloud infrastructure, authentication controls, transport security, and access restrictions appropriate to the service. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's privacy

University Hub is designed for students and general educational use, but it is not specifically directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information inappropriately, contact us so we can review and take reasonable steps.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect app changes, legal requirements, or operational needs. When we make material updates, we will revise the "Last updated" date on this page and may provide additional notice inside the app or on the website where appropriate.
